Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked to perfection. Known for its hearty and comforting flavors, lasagna is often seasoned with herbs like basil and oregano, making it a favorite for family meals and gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce (or Vegetarian Sauce Alternative):

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef, pork, or sausage (or plant-based alternative for vegetarian)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)

Other Ingredients

  • 1 package (9 oz) lasagna noodles (oven-ready or regular, cooked as per package instructions)
  • 2-3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • resh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)
